Each guide starts from a finished video and ends with your version of it. You don't write code and you don't learn a video editor. Your AI agent builds the video — you direct it: pick a guide, fill in your product's words, paste the prompt.

First time here? Do the one-time [Setup](/docs/guides/setup) — about ten minutes, all copy-paste.

## The guides

- [Launch video](/docs/guides/launch-video) — the ~35-second video we launched remocn with, rebuilt around your product.
