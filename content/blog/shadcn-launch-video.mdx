This one is a gift — an introducing spot for shadcn/ui made by a fan, not the owner. Fifty-five seconds, and by the end the viewer should know one thing: shadcn/ui is not a component library, it's how you build your own. remocn exists because of the shadcn model, so this video is also a thank-you note.

### The storyboard

1. **The component field** — a camera glide across a dense wall of real shadcn/ui components, installed from the registry and rendered live. No text for seven seconds. The product is the spectacle.
2. **The name** — "This is shadcn/ui" resolves alone, then the tagline gets its own beat.
3. **The creed** — two beats: "Not a component library" holds solo, then "How you build your own" assembles word by word. The negation creates the question; the next beat answers it.
4. **The montage** — four hard cuts, each a label plus live UI proving it: Blocks (a sidebar block assembling itself), Charts (a bar chart drawing live), Themes (a card hard-flipping dark → light → dark), Colors (the full Tailwind palette sweeping in as a wave).
5. **The pillars** — three claims stack as a block: accessible, composable, open code.
6. **The mechanism** — `npx shadcn add button` types itself, then the component name flips through the registry like a rolodex.
7. **The preset story** — the ui.shadcn.com/create builder flips Style, Base Color, Theme, and Font while a live sign-in card reshapes; the choices condense into one preset code; the code flips and the same installed components re-theme live. "Switch it any time."
8. **The ownership line** — "And the code is yours."
9. **The proof** — a count slams into 1,000,000 apps a month, a whip-pan carries into Startups / YC / Fortune 500s, and the kicker lands: "Everyone needs UI."
10. **The lockup** — a muted smoke ring, the name, "Open Source. Open Code." No URL anywhere. It's a gift, not an ad.

### Director's notes

**The hook is the product, not an illustration of it.** The opening wall and the category montage are real shadcn/ui components running live inside the video pipeline (Tailwind enabled in Remotion). Recognition does the persuading — you have met these cards in the wild.

**Color lives only where the brand allows it.** The video is monochrome — zinc-950, ink, Geist at regular weight only, nothing bolder anywhere. The only color in the whole piece sits inside the shader covers between scenes, and even that was sampled from shadcn's X avatar and heavily muted. Restraint is the brand.

**Every line is its own beat.** No headline ever carries a subtitle. A statement lands alone, plays its exit, and yields. That single rule is most of why the typography feels calm at fifty-five seconds.
